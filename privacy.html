Our Privacy Policy with Twitch API

We use Twitch API to provide our service using your Twitch account authenticated with us. As written above, we (SWA Associations / SeenWonderAlex) do not collect any data to the servers ourselves. When you authenticate using Google's OAuth2 screen, the authentication token provided by Twitch, that would be used serve its functionality, will be stored in your browser and no where else.

Usage of Twitch's Data

We make use of Twitch's Data for the following two services: Combined Twitch & YouTube Polls and Monitor Twitch Redemptions.

When you authenticate, we'll ask for one or more of the following permissions:

  • Manage your channelâ€™s polls.
  • Get details of your channel's polls.
  • View Channel Points rewards and their redemptions on your channel.

By default, all permissions are asked to simultaneously work between those two tools. However, if you plan or prefer to use one tool, you may right click (or hold on mobile) the Connect button to ask only the tool's required permissions.

For Combined Polls, we use these Poll Permissions in order to submit, manage, and delete polls to your Twitch streams.

For Monitor Twitch Redemptions, we use the Redemptions Read permission to monitor the Channel Point redemptions that were made in your channel and display them in the page (including if they were completed and refunded).

Removal of Usage of Twitch's Data

You may, at anytime, remove our usage of Twitch's Data, by navigating to Connected Accounts and clicking Disconnect. This will revoke and invalidate your Access Token to block our future access to your Twitch account, unless you re-authenticate with us.

NOTICE OF THE URL REDIRECT

You will be redirected to a different website by SWA Associations / SeenWonderAlex called seenwalex.wixsite.com. This is a backend server that we use to provide an Access Token and a Refresh Token. Access Token is the authentication token that the website will use to access your account, with Google API restrictions and the permission you approved. Refresh Token is the token that's used to extend the duration of the Access Token.

There is only one service that we make use of YouTube's Data, which is called Combined Twitch & YouTube Polls.
